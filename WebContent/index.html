Fast sloth club — Conformation show

The Fast Sloth Club is Brampton's newest organization dedicated to the sport of purebred dogs. Established in 2017, we are going to hold a famous all-breed, benched dog show every year at Brampton's Garden Square!

How a Dog Show Works

Each dog presented to a judge is exhibited (“handled”) by its owner, breeder, or a hired professional. Most dogs in competition at conformation shows are competing for points toward their championships. After these classes are judged, all the dogs that won first place in a class compete again to see who is the best of the winning dogs.

Rules

Class Dog vs Specialty Dog: Dogs working towards a Champion are a class dog, and Dogs working towards a Grand Champion are specialty Dogs. Dogs must complete Champion before they can start to work towards Grand Champion.
